About me

The CEO of Fresh Dew Concept is a fantastic coordinator of various entertainment outfit. The dream was born from a strong foundation for empowering the youth on the various field of entertainment which evolves around dance,promotion,event planning , decoration etc. He is a passionate writer, poet, veterinary teacher,loves reading,sightseeing,traveling,fish hunting. Ekumbe Ukel Ibor is his biological name but he's fondly called Fantastic Kummy. Born to a family of 13 in the central part of Cross River State precisely Mkpani. He celebrates his birthday every 27th May of a fresh year. Studied Diploma in Business Administration, University of Calabar and further to study English University of Lagos. His strong belief is rooted in the words of Pastor Paul Adefarasin in his church House on the Rock, where he has worshipped for 18 years running. Fresh Dew Concept has embarked on successful events such as " The Most Elegant Teenager pageant... Metil" " The Search for the Youngest Author.... Yaps" " The Heritage" " Series" " The Young Writers Club" " Yada" just to mention a few. His dance crew Dew Crusher has groom and inspired many dancers nation wide and his flare for jokes has also given birth the numerous comedians in the likes of DvD, Bonus Picken, Daady Jay, Tonny Wonder, Zikky. Fresh Dew Concept is a foundational platform that promotes ideas and inculgates discipline, intergrity, honesty, objectivity, productive and projectivity.
